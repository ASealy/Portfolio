Andrew Sealy

Computer Science Student & Intern

About

Andrew is a 4th year Computer Science student at Dalhousie University. Andrew has previously worked as a Software Developer at Health QR where he contributed to the ongoing development of a medication management application for prescription patients. Andrew has most recently worked as a Business Technology Analyst at Deloitte. There, he was involved in several client engagements as a member of the technology consultancy. Through his academic and professional career, Andrew has gained experience in several modern development frameworks and programming languages and he specializes in the development of web applications. Andrew is also involved with several volunteering engagements throughout his community including Halifax Youth Council and Halifax Street Soccer. Andrew was recently the recipient of the Leadership Award for Social Responsibility from Deloitte’s Business Analysts Action Committee.
